All Attractions

Comprehensive information on tourist attractions, opening hours, and travel directions, all in one place.

1. Chiang Khan Walking Street

Chiang Khan Walking Street, or Chai Khong Road, is located in Chiang Khan district, about 47 km from Loei city. It is a night market along the Mekong River, lined with wooden houses over 100 years old.

Open: Daily 17.00-22.00

Address: Chai Khong Rd, Chiang Khan, Loei 42110

Parking: Wat Tha Khok or nearby private lots

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 47 km

Travel time: 50 mins by car

2. Phu Thok (Chiang Khan)

Phu Thok involves a high mountain located by the Mekong River. It is one of the most beautiful viewpoints for the sea of mist in Chiang Khan, perfect for sunrise and panoramic river views.

Open: Daily 05.00-17.00

Address: Chiang Khan, Loei 42110

Fee: Shuttle bus 20-30 THB/person

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 52 km

Travel time: 55 mins by car

3. Skywalk Chiang Khan (Phu Khok Ngio)

Located in Pak Tom, Chiang Khan, at the meeting point of the Hueang and Mekong rivers. The glass walkway is about 80 meters above the Mekong River level.

Open: Daily 07.00-18.00

Address: Ban Tha Di Mi, Pak Tom, Chiang Khan, Loei

Fee: Shuttle + Shoe covers 60 THB

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 70 km

Travel time: 1 hour 10 mins

4. Kaeng Khut Khu

A large rapid area in the Mekong River with many large rocks. The strong currents make it a sight to behold. Best visited in the dry season (Feb-May) for boat trips.

Open: 08.00-18.00

Address: Chiang Khan, Loei

Parking: Ample parking available

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 50 km

Travel time: 55 mins

5. Phu Ruea National Park

"The coldest place in Siam." The summit offers cold weather year-round and is a beautiful sunrise spot with many winter flowers.

Open: 05.00-18.00 (Viewpoint)

Address: Phu Ruea, Loei

Fee: Adult 40 THB, Child 20 THB

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 50 km

Travel time: 1 hour

6. Phu Kradueng

A legendary mountain for trekkers. A test of strength to hike up. Famous for sunrise at Pha Nok Aen and sunset at Pha Lom Sak.

Open: Oct - May (Closed in rainy season)

Address: Phu Kradueng, Loei

Fee: Park fee + Porter fee (optional)

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 75 km

Travel time: 1 hour 15 mins

7. Suan Hin Pha Ngam

Known as "Kunming of Loei," featuring complex and beautiful limestone cliffs like a fairytale mountain, with exciting nature trails.

Open: 08.00-18.00

Address: Nong Hin, Loei

Fee: Shuttle tractor 20-30 THB

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 45 km

Travel time: 45 mins

8. Phu Pa Poh (Fuji of Loei)

A viewpoint overlooking Phu Ho, which resembles Mount Fuji. Visitors take a local tractor up to see the 360-degree view.

Open: 05.00-18.00

Address: Puan Phu, Nong Hin, Loei

Fee: Tractor fee 50-60 THB

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 45 km

Travel time: 45 mins (Near Suan Hin)

9. Phra That Sri Song Rak

A sacred stupa symbolizing friendship and unity. Caution: Do not wear red clothing within the stupa area.

Open: 08.00-16.30

Address: Dan Sai, Loei

Rule: No red clothing allowed

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 83 km

Travel time: 1 hour 20 mins

10. Phi Ta Khon Museum

Located at Wat Phon Chai in Dan Sai. It exhibits the history of the Phi Ta Khon festival, models, and mask-painting demonstrations.

Open: 08.00-17.00

Address: Wat Phon Chai, Dan Sai, Loei

Fee: Free entry

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 83 km

Travel time: 1 hour 20 mins

11. Huai Krathing Reservoir

A natural tourist spot with a peaceful atmosphere. Perfect for bamboo rafting, dining, and relaxing with family.

Open: 09.00-18.00

Address: Mueang Loei, Loei

Activity: Rafting, Dining

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 25 km

Travel time: 30 mins

12. Wat Neramit Wipatsana

A beautiful temple on a hill, built entirely of laterite stone. Inside houses a replica of Phra Buddha Chinnarat.

Open: 06.00-18.00

Address: Dan Sai, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 82 km

Travel time: 1 hour 20 mins

13. Phu Luang Wildlife Sanctuary

Known as the "Emerald of Isan," featuring rich nature, lush forests, and various wild orchids (especially white rhododendrons). Cool weather all year.

Open: 08.00-16.00 (Check seasons)

Address: Phu Ruea, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 40 km

Travel time: 45 mins

14. Pla Ba Waterfall

A beautiful waterfall surrounded by nature, suitable for swimming and picnicking. Peaceful atmosphere near Phu Ruea.

Open: 08.00-17.00

Address: Ban Pla Ba, Phu Ruea, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 60 km

Travel time: 1 hour

15. Phu Pha Man National Park

Outstanding limestone cliffs against the sky. The highlight is the bat cave with millions of bats flying out in a continuous stream during the evening.

Open: 06.00-18.00

Address: Phu Pha Man, Khon Kaen (Connects to Loei)

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 80 km

Travel time: 1 hour 15 mins

16. Phra That Satja

A sacred stupa of Tha Li people, built in the style of Phra That Phanom. The white stupa with gold motifs is very beautiful.

Open: 06.00-18.00

Address: Wat Lat Pu Song Tham, Tha Li, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 45 km

Travel time: 50 mins

17. Wat Pa Huay Lad

A monastery with many beautiful sculptures, including Naga, Trimurti, and various sacred items. Peaceful atmosphere.

Open: 07.00-18.00

Address: Phu Ruea, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 55 km

Travel time: 1 hour

18. TSA Flower Garden

A winter flower garden with various species. A popular check-in spot for photos in winter (Open only in season).

Open: 08.00-17.00 (Check before visit)

Address: Phu Ruea, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 52 km

Travel time: 55 mins

19. Kut Pong Public Park

The lung of the city. Shady atmosphere with large trees. Suitable for walking, exercising, or picknicking in the evening.

Open: 05.00-20.00

Address: Kut Pong, Mueang Loei (Behind City Pillar Shrine)

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 0 km

Travel time: 1-5 mins

20. Loei Night Bazaar

A hub for local food, clothes, and souvenirs with friendly prices. Lively atmosphere at night in the city center.

Open: 17.00-22.00

Address: Mueang Loei, Loei

Google Map: View Map

Distance from city: 0 km

Travel time: 5 mins
